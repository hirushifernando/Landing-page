About Us

Since its inception in 1954, Burger Kingdom has been a pioneer in the fast-food industry, revolutionizing the way people enjoy burgers worldwide. With a relentless commitment to quality and innovation, Burger Kingdom has established itself as an iconic brand synonymous with delicious flame-grilled burgers, fresh ingredients, and unparalleled taste. From our humble beginnings in Miami, Florida, Burger Kingdom has grown into a global phenomenon, serving millions of customers every day across the globe. Our unwavering dedication to providing exceptional dining experiences and our passion for crafting the perfect burger have cemented Burger Kingdom as a household name, cherished by burger enthusiasts of all ages. With a rich heritage spanning decades, we continue to push the boundaries of flavor and convenience, ensuring that every bite at Burger Kingdom is an unforgettable delight.

Our Certifications

At Burger Kingdom, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality assurance and product safety across all our offerings.
